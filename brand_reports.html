Analysis & Recommendations

Performance Trends

  • Video Dominance: Reels, especially the "People of Satwik" series, are the primary driver of views and interaction on Instagram and Facebook.
  • Strong Organic Foundation: The current strategy is successfully building a genuine audience without reliance on paid ads, proving content-market fit.
  • Platform-Specific Content: While video excels on Meta platforms, document posts are showing engagement potential on LinkedIn.

Key Takeaways

  • Double Down on Video: Prioritize and expand the "People of Satwik" series. This authentic content clearly resonates with the audience.
  • Maintain Organic Strategy: Continue focusing on high-quality, engaging content to grow reach cost-effectively. The data proves it works.
  • Tailor for LinkedIn: Explore creating more insightful document posts or carousels for the professional audience on LinkedIn to build authority.

Content & Design Report

Focus: Content Pillars & Creative Assets

Social Media Content Pillars

1. Informative Reels

This content educates users on our core processes, building trust through transparency. We focus on our 6-step mechanized process, hygiene standards, post-service certification, and the use of UV treatment to ensure healthy, germ-free water.

2. People of Satvik Series

This series humanizes the brand by featuring stories from our team—from the owners to our skilled technicians. We share how Satwik was established in 2006 and grew to serve key areas like Malviya Nagar and Tonk Phatak, catering to households, hotels, and corporate clients.

4. Story of Satvik & Daily Life Reels

This content offers a behind-the-scenes look at our daily operations. We showcase our teamwork, the meticulous planning before a cleaning job, and discussions on how to best serve our clients, highlighting the dedication involved in our daily work.

3. Myth Buster Content

We address common misconceptions about water purity, cleaning technology, and hygiene standards. This positions Satwik as an industry expert and a reliable source of information for our audience.

Brand Creatives

In addition to social media content, we developed key branding materials. This included designing professional labels for our cleaning products and creating visually engaging event standees and flyers to represent the brand offline.

Website Technical Report

Focus: Critical Issues & Campaign Impact

Critical SSL Certificate Issue

Problem Analysis

The website is currently inaccessible to many users due to a missing or expired SSL certificate. This results in browsers showing a "Not Secure" warning, preventing visitors from proceeding to the site. This issue has had a direct and significant negative impact on our marketing campaigns, especially the GMB profile which directs users to the website.

Impact on GMB Campaign

The GMB campaign's effectiveness was severely hampered. Clicks from the Google Business Profile led to a security warning instead of the website, causing a sharp drop in traffic and potential leads. As a result, the campaign impressions and engagement went down significantly. It is crucial to resolve this SSL issue before launching any new campaigns to ensure marketing spend is not wasted.

Brand Marketing Report

Focus: GMB, SEO & Ad Performance

Foundational Marketing Initiatives

GMB Business Profile Setup

We established and fully set up the Google Business Profile for Satwik Services. This is a critical step for local visibility, ensuring the business appears in Google Maps and local search results for relevant queries.

SEO Optimization

We performed on-page and local SEO optimization to improve the GMB listing's rank. This included targeted keyword research, crafting a compelling business description, selecting appropriate service categories, and ensuring all information was accurate and consistent to build trust with Google's algorithm.
